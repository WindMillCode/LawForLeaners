About Us
The Law for Learners “ignorantia juris non excusat” – “ignorance of the law excuses not"
The Law for Learners has been created for the purpose of familiarizing everyone with the laws in a new easy way which is absolutely free.
We thought of coming up with a platform for you where you can acquaint yourself with various national and international laws, academic law courses, deeper dive into legal issues in a creative and exciting ways.

We have designed our online lectures and courses in such a way where people from both legal and non-legal background can gain knowledge andbecome more informed about these laws and its practical implications.

We hope this platform will benefit the mass including but not limited to law students, teachers and legal practitioners in expanding their knowledges and skills in legal learnings
